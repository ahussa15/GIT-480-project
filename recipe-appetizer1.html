Ingredients:

  • 1 lb fresh Edamame in pods
  • Sea salt for sprinkling

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  2. Add the Edamame pods to the boiling water.
  3. Cook for 3-5 minutes or until the Edamame is tender.
  4. Drain the Edamame and transfer them to a bowl.
  5. Sprinkle sea salt over the Edamame and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
