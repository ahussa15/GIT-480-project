Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons matcha powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, matcha powder, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  3. Once churned, transfer the ice cream to a lidded container and freeze until firm.
  4. Scoop and serve the delicious matcha ice cream!
