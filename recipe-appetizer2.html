Ingredients:

  • 4 cups dashi (Japanese soup stock)
  • 1/2 cup miso paste
  • 1 cup tofu, diced
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Seaweed (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat dashi in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Dissolve miso paste in a small bowl with some dashi.
  3. Add the miso mixture to the pot and stir well.
  4. Add tofu and cook until heated through.
  5. Top with green onions and seaweed if desired.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade miso soup!
