Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • Nori (seaweed) sheets
  • Sashimi-grade fish (tuna, salmon, etc.)
  • Vegetables (cucumber, avocado, etc.)
  • Soy sauce for dipping
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger for serving

Instructions:

  1. Cook sushi rice according to package instructions.
  2. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi rolling mat.
  3. Wet your hands and spread a thin layer of rice on the nori, leaving a border at the top.
  4. Arrange fish and vegetables in the center of the rice.
  5. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat.
  6. Cut the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces.
  7. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
  8. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
  9. Enjoy your delicious homemade sushi platter!
