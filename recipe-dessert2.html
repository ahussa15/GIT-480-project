Ingredients:

  • 1 cup glutinous rice flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sweetened red bean paste
  • Cornstarch for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix glutinous rice flour, sugar, and water until smooth.
  2. Cook the mixture in a microwave or on the stovetop until it thickens and becomes translucent.
  3. Allow the mixture to cool, then divide it into small portions.
  4. Flatten each portion, add a spoonful of sweetened red bean paste, and seal the edges to form mochi balls.
  5. Dust the mochi with cornstarch to prevent sticking.
  6. Serve and enjoy the delightful sweetness of homemade mochi!
